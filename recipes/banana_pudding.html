Banana Pudding:

Ingredients:

8 oz cream cheese, softened to room temperature

14 oz sweetened condensed milk

5 oz package INSTANT vanilla pudding mix (larger box)

3 cups milk (2% or higher)

2 tsp vanilla extract

8 oz tub whipped topping (COOL WHIP), thawed (divided use)

11 oz box Nilla wafers (divided use)

5–7 bananas, peeled and sliced (divided use)

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth and fluffy.

2. Add sweetened condensed milk, vanilla pudding mix, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

3. Stir in half the whipped topping.

4. Layer one-third of the Nilla wafers on the bottom of a trifle dish, large bowl, or 9×13-inch pan.

5. Add a single layer of sliced bananas.

6. Pour one-third of the pudding mixture on top (enough to cover all the bananas).

7. Repeat layering two more times: wafers, bananas, pudding.

8. Top with the remaining whipped topping.

9. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1–2 hours until thickened and the wafers soften.
